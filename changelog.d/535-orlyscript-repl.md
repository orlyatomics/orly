- **Added**: `orly-repl` ([`clients/repl`](https://github.com/orlyatomics/orly/tree/master/clients/repl)) — an interactive orlyscript REPL against a running `orlyi`: expressions evaluate in place, definitions accumulate and are callable from later entries, and writes go through a real POV (`--shared` / `--pov <id>` to join one agents are writing). Each entry is folded into a synthetic package, compiled with `orlyc`, and installed at the next version behind the scenes; compile errors surface the orlyc/g++ diagnostics and leave the session intact. CI-gated by a stdin-driven smoke test (#535).
